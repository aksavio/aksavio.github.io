Ingredients

  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin
  • Chilies(there are many to choose from and vary the amount to your liking)
  • eggs
  • Spinach/baby spinach
  • butter, oil, seasoning(salt, pepper)


Directions

  1. put some oil in a pan, let it get hot and add some cumin and mustard seeds, let them roast for a few seconds
  2. add chopped Chilies
  3. keep some thinly sliced spinach and add them to the pan roughly covering the pan
  4. add a few knobs of butter and then add your whisked eggs.
  5. cook till the bottom is crisp and the top has become soft and no longer watery.
